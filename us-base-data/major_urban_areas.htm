Abstract:
U.S. Urbanized Areas represents boundaries for urban areas with a population greater than 50,000. The data set covers the 50 States plus the District of Columbia within United States.
Purpose:
U.S. Urbanized Areas provides information about the locations, names, and urban codes of urbanized areas primarily for national planning applications.
